Guess the Oscar Winners

Do you know who will win Best Picture, Costume Design, or Cinematography?

In this game, you will select the nominee who you think will win in their specific category.

After making your selections for each and every category there is one more thing, you will select a confidence level for each category. This confidence level will determine how many points you are awarded for selecting the correct winner in that category.

Good luck! And remember to watch live on Oscar Sunday to see how well you did.

Now that you made your picks, how confident are you?

On the next screen you will choose your confidence level that your selection will win their category.

You will drag and drop your choices to set your pick next to your confidence score. The points range from 24 points (most confident) down to 1 point (least confident).

Once you have moved all of your picks to their desired score, submit your answers. If you provide me with your name and email, I will sent you an email with your selections so that you can follow along on Oscar Sunday.
